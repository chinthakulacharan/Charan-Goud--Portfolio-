How I Think

My PM Approach —

🔍
Problem First, Always
I start by deeply understanding the user problem before jumping to solutions. A well-defined problem is already 50% solved. I use 5 Whys, user interviews, and data to get to root causes.
📊
Data-Driven Decisions
Every product decision should be backed by data — quantitative metrics (retention, conversion, churn) and qualitative signals (user feedback, reviews). I use Mixpanel to validate hypotheses.
🚀
Ship, Learn, Iterate
I believe in building lean MVPs, shipping early to real users, and iterating fast based on feedback. Perfect is the enemy of shipped — I prioritize learning velocity over feature completeness.
👥
User Empathy at Core
Great products come from deeply understanding users — their jobs-to-be-done, pain points, and mental models. I build detailed user personas to keep teams aligned on who we're building for.
🤖
AI-Powered Product Thinking
AI is changing what's possible. I stay ahead by understanding LLMs, generative AI capabilities, and how to build AI-first user experiences that feel magical yet trustworthy.
⚖️
Ruthless Prioritization
With limited resources, prioritization is a superpower. I use RICE, MoSCoW, and Impact/Effort matrices to decide what to build now, next, and never — and communicate the why clearly.

My PM Project —

LIVE CASE STUDY
📦 Q-Commerce · Mar – Apr 2026

Ensure Accurate Orders in
Food Delivery (Blinkit Case Study)

Designed a complete PM case study for Blinkit addressing its #1 customer complaint — missing or incorrect items affecting 12% of daily orders across 5 lakh+ deliveries.

30%Missing Item Reduction
₹2.7CrRefund Savings / Month
95%+Order Accuracy Goal
🔍 Problem
1 in 5 negative reviews cited wrong or incomplete orders, causing refunds, lost trust, and churn across 5 lakh+ daily deliveries.
📊 Research
Analyzed Play Store reviews, root cause analysis identifying 5 systemic gaps, competitive analysis across Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart & BigBasket.
💡 Solution
Designed Order Verification MVP — item-level QR scanning, delivery partner photo confirmation, customer-visible order proof, one-tap dispute flow.
🧪 Experiment
A/B test across top 5 dark stores over 4 weeks. Success = 30% drop in complaints + 95%+ order accuracy.
📌 Deliverables
Full PRD, P1/P2/P3 prioritization, 5 prototype screenshots, metrics dashboard, risks & mitigation plan.
